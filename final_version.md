### Area of Focus: Program Management in International Development
International development programs operate in high-complexity environments — spanning multiple countries, funding agencies, partner organizations, and reporting requirements. Effective program management in this space demands clear communication, rigorous monitoring and evaluation, and the ability to synthesize large volumes of information into actionable decisions. Because of this complexity, international development program management is a strong use case for exploring how generative AI can support human decision-making.

### Three Ways Generative AI Could Help
- **Automated reporting and documentation:** Generative AI can draft donor reports, progress updates, and meeting summaries from structured data inputs, significantly reducing the time program staff spend on routine writing tasks.
- **Cross-team coordination support:** AI tools can synthesize updates from multiple workstreams, flag misalignments or dependencies, and generate clear briefing materials — helping program managers maintain situational awareness across complex, distributed teams.
- **Knowledge management and institutional memory:** In organizations with high staff turnover, generative AI can help capture, organize, and surface institutional knowledge, making it easier to onboard new team members and maintain continuity across program cycles.
